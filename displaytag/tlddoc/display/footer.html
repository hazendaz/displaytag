Tag footer

Tag wich should be nested into a table tag to provide a custom table footer. The body of the tag is into the tfoot section of the table. The totals variable, if designated, will be in pageContext in this tag.

Example: 
      
<display:table name="someList" varTotals="totals">
  <display:column property="itemName"/>
  <display:column property="price" total="true"/>
  <display:footer>
    <tr>
      <td>Total Bill:</td>
      <td>${totals.column2}</td>
    </tr>
  </display:footer>
</display:table>
